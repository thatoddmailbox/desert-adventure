You go door to door, desperate to find shelter before the fast-approaching desert night. You are rejected at every door, until, as night begins to decend and you begin to surrender hope, a kindly old woman allows you to spend the night in her house. She feeds you well and manages to eat more than you would expect of a woman her size. After dinner, she leads you to a guest room. As you lay down in bed and give yourself up to sleep, you admire the moon and the stars outside your window.

However, in the middle of the night, you are suddenly awoken to a growling. You get out of bed to find the old woman has become a werewolf! What do you do?
  • Fight her!
  • Run away!
  • Ally with her!
